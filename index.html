Lucky Duck

An Original Tarot Deck Based On Real Duck Memes

How To Play

*Toggle Ctrl + and Ctrl - on Desktop for optimal alignment*

At the start of the game, two players are given 3 random Lucky Duck Tarot cards faced down.
Each card represents their past, present, and future respectively.
The Luckier Duck is the player with the highest Total Luck.
Total Luck = Present - Past + Future
1. Player 1 and Player 2 will take turns to decide their fate.
2. Both Players will each have 3 turns; one for their past card, present card and future card.
3. During each turn, the player can shuffle, or keep the card they currently have.
4. Once a turn has passed the player can no longer trade their card and the turn's card will be revealed.
5. The Luckier Duck will receive a fortune!
Click Restart game to reset game mode.
